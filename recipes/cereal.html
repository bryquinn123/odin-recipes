Cereal Recipe

Description

To make a delicious bowl of cereal, you will need your favorite cereal and cold milk.

This bowl of cereal will be the perfect breakfast to start the day.

  • Cereal of Choice
  • Cold Milk
  • Bowl and Spoon

Steps

  1. Open Box of Cereal
  2. Pour Cereal into Bowl
  3. Pour Milk into Bowl
  4. Eat Cereal
