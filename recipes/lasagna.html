Lasagna Recipe

Description

To make a delicious lasagna, start with great ingredients.

This recipe will have you wanting to make it over and over again.

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Start by boiling water and cooking lasagna sheets according to package instructions
  3. While pasta is cooking, brown the ground beef in a pan
  4. Once ground beef is browned, pour in tomato sauce
  5. When pasta is finished cooking, spread a layer of sauce, then a layer of lasagna sheets, then a layer of ricotta
  6. Repeat layering three times
  7. Top with mozzarella
  8. Cook in oven for 20 minutes covered, then another 20 minutes uncovered
  9. Remove and Serve
