Pizza Recipe

Description

This guide will talk you through how to make amazing pizza.

You'll need an oven capable of reaching very high temperatures.

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 700 degrees
  2. Roll out pizza dough into desired shape
  3. Spread sauce over dough
  4. Cover with cheese and toppings
  5. Cook in oven for 3 minutes until cheese begins to bubble
  6. Remove from oven and let cool 5 moinutes
